Brand Story

Shin Ramyun, launched by Nongshim in October 1986, quickly became a popular instant noodle by highlighting a bold spicy profile and rich broth flavor. The character "辛" in its name became a memorable symbol of heat and identity for the brand. Over time, it maintained strong recognition in Korea's noodle market while continuously expanding into new product lines. Its long-standing trust and mainstream appeal are key reasons it remains a top choice today.
